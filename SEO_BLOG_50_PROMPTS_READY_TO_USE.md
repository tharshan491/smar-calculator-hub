## Additional Tips for Generating Content:

### Style Guidelines:
- ✅ Write like you're teaching a friend, not a textbook
- ✅ Use real numbers from real businesses/lives
- ✅ Include "wrong vs right" examples
- ✅ Start with problems people have, not concepts

### Structure Consistency:
- Use H2 for main section headers
- Use H3 for subsections
- Include 1-2 real-number examples per section
- Place calculator CTA after each major section (soft) or in conclusion (hard)
- Include at least 5 calculator references throughout

### SEO Implementation:
- Put keywords in: Title (once), Headers (2-3 times), Meta descriptions, FAQ questions
- Internal links: to blog page, other related articles
- External signals: mention authority sources (WHO, CDC, Financial Times, etc)
- Structure: People ask "How to..." - answer it in headers

### Real Numbers Matter:
- $250-900 annual savings
- 30% off means $30 savings on $100
- 5% interest on $10,000 = $500/year
- Sleep cycles: 90 minutes per cycle
- These examples stick in readers' minds

### Call-to-Action Placement:
1. After problem statement (soft): "Our [calculator] takes the guesswork out..."
2. After formula (soft): "Don't calculate manually..."
3. After examples (hard): **[Gradient button]** "Calculate [Result]"
4. After FAQ (soft, in answers): "Use our calculator for instant result..."
5. Conclusion (hard): **[Large button]** "Get Your [Result] Now"
