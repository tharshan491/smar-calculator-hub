## H1: Age Calculator - Find Out Your Exact Age

### What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a tool that determines your exact age based on your birth date. It calculates not just years, but also remaining months, days, and even hours until your next birthday. It's useful for various purposes from entertainment to official documentation.

### How to Use the Age Calculator

**Step 1: Enter Birth Date**
Input your date of birth using the date picker or type manually (MM/DD/YYYY format).

**Step 2: Select Current Date**
The calculator defaults to today, but you can select any date to calculate age at that specific time.

**Step 3: Calculate**
Click the calculate button to see your age in multiple formats.

**Step 4: View Results**
See your age displayed in years, months, days, hours, and total days lived.

### Understanding Age Calculator Results

**Years Old**
Your age in complete years. This is the standard age metric used for birthdays and official documents.

**Months and Days**
How many months and days beyond your complete years. For example, 25 years, 3 months, and 17 days.

**Total Months Lived**
Your age expressed entirely in months. Useful for tracking babies' ages or specific medical/legal purposes.

**Total Days Lived**
The complete number of days from birth to today. Interesting perspective on how long you've actually been alive.

**Hours Lived**
The number of hours from birth to the current moment. Provides dramatic perspective on time!

**Next Birthday**
Days remaining until your next birthday. Countdown to your special day.

### Age Calculator FAQs

**Q: Why do my results show months beyond 12?**
A: They shouldn't! We show years, months (0-11), and days. If you see differently, let us know.

**Q: Is the calculator accurate?**
A: Our calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths. It's as accurate as atomic time standards!

**Q: Can I calculate someone else's age?**
A: Yes! Enter anyone's birth date. Useful for calculating celebrity ages or historical figures.

**Q: How do leap years affect age calculation?**
A: Our calculator accounts for all leap years. People born Feb 29 get special treats!

**Q: Can I export my age calculation?**
A: Yes, use the download button to save results as a file.

**Q: What if I don't know the exact birth time?**
A: Age in years, months, and days doesn't require exact time. Time-based calculations use 12:00 PM as default.
